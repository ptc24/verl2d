Description

This is based on verl2d, with the addition of an "object". The particles in the object are connected by springs that obey Hooke's law (measuring the length of the spring from the centres of the particles). Note that the angles of the springs are not fixed - try setting the number of rows or columns to one, to get a flexible "chain" of particles.
